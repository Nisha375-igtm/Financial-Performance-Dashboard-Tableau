# Financial-Performance-Dashboard-Tableau
Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing financial performance across products, countries, and segments using sales, profit, and discount data.

## Dataset Fields
Segment, Country, Product, Discount Band, Units Sold, Manufacturing Price, Sale Price, Gross Sales, Discounts, Sales, COGS, Profit, Date, Month, Year

## Dashboard Sheets & Visuals
Sheet   Chart Type  Metrics Analyzed 
Sheet 1 Bar Chart   Profit & Sales by Country, Product, Segment
Sheet 2 Line Chart  Monthly & Yearly Profit and Sales trends by Segment
Sheet 3 Chart        Gross Sales vs Discounts by Product
Sheet 4 Chart       Sales by Product and Discount Band

## Tools Used
-Tableau Public

-Microsoft Excel (source data)

## Key Learnings
-Analyzed how discount bands impact net sales and profitability across products

-Identified top-performing segments and countries by revenue and profit

-Built trend analysis to track monthly and yearly financial performance
